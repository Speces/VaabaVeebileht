Taste the Feeling
Coca-Cola
What it is:
Coca-Cola is a classic cola soft drink known worldwide for its timeless taste and iconic brand.
Taste:
It has a deep, caramel-like flavour with balanced sweetness and a refreshing fizzy finish.
History:
Created in 1886, Coca-Cola grew from a small pharmacy drink into one of the most recognizable beverages on the planet.
Brand personality:
Coca-Cola stands for happiness, sharing, and togetherness. Its style is warm, nostalgic, and universal.
Fun fact:
The recipe for Coca-Cola's syrup is famously kept locked in a vault.
